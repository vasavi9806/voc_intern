Before You Write In

Quick answers

What's your typical turnaround?

Short form edits usually turn around in 24–48 hours. Long form and documentary-style pieces run 3–7 days depending on length and revision rounds.

Do you work with raw, unedited footage?

Yes — most clients send raw footage or screen recordings directly. We handle syncing, selects, and organization as part of the edit.

Can you manage posting, not just editing?

Yes. Social strategy and scheduling can be bundled with editing, and paid ads management is available as a separate add-on.

How do revisions work?

You leave timestamped notes on the draft; two revision rounds are included in every project, with additional rounds billed hourly.
