A small studio that treats editing like strategy, not finishing.

Editkaro.in started as a two-person freelance editing hustle and grew into a full social + video team without losing the part where an actual human watches your footage before touching it.

Mission

Make every video earn its watch time.

We exist to close the gap between "we posted it" and "people watched it." That means editing, grading and sound decisions made for retention and platform behavior — not just a clean timeline.

Vision

Become the edit bay brands don't outgrow.

We want teams to bring us their footage on day one and still be sending it to us at year five — because the pipeline scales with them, from a single reel to a full content calendar.

How We Got Here

A short timeline of a studio that grew by referral.

  • 2021

    Freelance editing, one client at a time

    Started editing gaming montages and short-form clips for creators, one project at a time, no studio, no team.

  • 2022

    First retainer clients

    Moved from one-off edits to monthly retainers as eCommerce and football-league clients needed a steady output.

  • 2023

    Built out color and sound

    Brought on a dedicated colorist and sound designer so grading and mix stopped being an afterthought.

  • 2024

    Added paid ads & strategy

    Started managing the media spend behind the edits, closing the loop between the cut and the conversion.

  • Today

    A nine-format studio

    Short form to anime AMVs — same pipeline, same 36-hour turnaround, a bigger room of specialists.
